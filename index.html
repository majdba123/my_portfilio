About Me

We can make it together

I obtained a Bachelor's degree in information technology with a specialization in Software Engineering from the University of Al-Sham Private. During my studies, I gained in-depth knowledge in software design and development, software engineering principles, and database management. I participated in several practical projects related to web and mobile application development, which enhanced my technical and analytical skills

I design and implement scalable backend solutions that meet user needs while ensuring high performance and reliability. My collaborative approach with cross-functional teams ensures smooth integration of services and optimal functionality of software solutions.
